3rd International Workshop on Engineering IoT Systems:
Architectures, Services, Applications, and Platforms

March 25, 2019, Hamburg, Germany - co-located with ICSA 2019

News:

January 18, 2019: The submission deadline has been extended to January 22, AoE!


To exploit all the benefits of the Internet of Things (IoT), a wide range of challenges needs to be addressed. The objective of IoT-ASAP 2019, Third International Workshop on Engineering IoT systems: Architectures, Services, Applications, and Platforms, is again to bring together researchers and practitioners from several areas (e.g., Architecture, Internet of Things (IoT), Service-Oriented Computing, Self-Adaptive Systems, Multi-Agent Systems, Data Analytics, User Interaction and Experience) to investigate and discuss the latest R&D trends, principles, challenges of, and (interdisciplinary) approaches for engineering IoT systems.

This year, the IoT-ASAP program plans to be even more interactive than the previous years, with some paper presentations and a big focus on break-out sessions. A keynote addressing the key topics of the workshop will provide a good basis for discussions. The program plans to attract both academics and practitioners.
